New Oriental Training School

New Oriental Education Technology Group is positioned as a comprehensive education group with the comprehensive growth of students as the core and science and technology as the driving force.

The Group has developed from Beijing New Oriental School established in 1993, and has a number of development platforms such as short-term training system, basic education system, cultural dissemination system, consulting service system, science and technology industry system, and venture capital ecosystem.

It has created many well-known education brands such as New Oriental Primary and Secondary School General Education, New Oriental Primary and Secondary School International Education, New Oriental Study Abroad Examination, New Oriental University Student Learning and Development Center, New Oriental Online, New Oriental Future Abroad, New Oriental International Study Tour, New Oriental Full of Stars, New Oriental Big Fool Culture, New Oriental International Bilingual School and so on.

As a well-known private educational institution in China, New Oriental Education Technology Group was successfully listed on the New York Stock Exchange on September 7, 2006 and re-listed on the Hong Kong Stock Exchange on November 9, 2020. As of November 30, 2020, New Oriental has set up 117 schools, 12 bookstores and 1,518 learning centers in 103 cities across the country, with more than 45,700 teachers and more than 62.6 million face-to-face students.

This is the profile of our group.
